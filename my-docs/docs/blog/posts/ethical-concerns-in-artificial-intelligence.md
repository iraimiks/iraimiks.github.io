# Ethical concerns in artificial intelligence

There are no ethical concerns—AI is a new technology, and we all need to adapt to it, understand how it works, and figure out how to make the best use of it. I feel that, in some cases, AI gives us the ability to do things faster. However, in other ways, it can slow things down by generating opinions or outputs that I need to review and understand before applying them to the real world.
AI is a model that imitates the real world in text or, in some cases, a virtual environment. Go.
