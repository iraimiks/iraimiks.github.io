# The role of big data in decision-making

Hope that, someday, it will give us the ability to create the greatest possible option where everyone is safe. Right now, it seems like everything is transitioning into a digital environment. But I feel that true value comes from the stories humans share with each other.
From what I understand, big data refers to either vast amounts of similar data from many people or a large collection of varied data in one place, where decisions must be made. As I see it, these systems are designed to continuously analyze, summarize, and make decisions.
