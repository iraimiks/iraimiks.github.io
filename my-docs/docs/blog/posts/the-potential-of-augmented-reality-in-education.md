# The potential of augmented reality in education

I don’t want my kids to use technology until they reach a point in their lives where they themselves want to use it. However, if we’re talking about explaining certain concepts, augmented reality can be helpful. Sometimes, words alone aren’t enough to explain things, and this visual tool can provide a better understanding.
I think real-life experiences, with all the senses we have, are much better tools than technological additions like augmented reality. But in some cases, augmented reality can truly help improve understanding. Each technology has its place. Go.
