# The future of autonomous vehicles

Someday, I want a vehicle that can take me from point A to point B. That would be a great feeling. However, it seems the technology is still far behind the ideal conditions needed to provide safe driving across the entire country.
I live in Latvia, and I’m not sure if autonomous driving will arrive here in the near future. There are many issues with the roads and driving culture—it’s not bad, but there’s definitely room for improvement. I’m not sure if autonomous vehicles would perform well on our roads.
