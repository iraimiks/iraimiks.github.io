# The growth of IoT (Internet of Things) in homes

I think smart home technology is still at a hobbyist level—there needs to be 100% trust before people feel comfortable controlling different parts of their home environment. While it’s cool to have video cameras that let you check on things at home, there’s still a huge lack of trust when it comes to security and physical privacy.
In general, I use a vacuum robot for cleaning and have an air quality device that improves the air in my home. It’s great that you can control these devices using your phone.
