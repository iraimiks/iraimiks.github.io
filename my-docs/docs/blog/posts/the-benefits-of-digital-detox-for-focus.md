# The benefits of digital detox for focus

I have a lot of screen time, and I know that a detox is 100% good for my mental and physical health. I believe that every break I take is beneficial for me, especially as someone who relies heavily on brainpower for work.
I try to use my phone as little as possible. Sometimes, I catch myself doomscrolling, but I do my best to manage it. What helps me most, as I see it, is having an internal dialogue about the actions I’m taking. I can help myself by asking the right questions: Is this good for me? How will it impact my future?
