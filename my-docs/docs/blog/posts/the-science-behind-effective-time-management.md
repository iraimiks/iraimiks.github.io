# The science behind effective time management

A planner seems like a good tool for effectively managing time. I think you need to have a sense of how many tasks or actions you want to accomplish. Sometimes, setting a time limit is great, but in some processes, a time cap isn’t necessary—you just focus on completing the work until it’s done.
I believe it can be effective to measure each task from start to finish and then review how well you performed. I think the attitude of trying to complete tasks as quickly as possible can be beneficial.
