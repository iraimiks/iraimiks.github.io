# The evolution of smartphones over the past decade

I use a phone that I bought from my brother—it’s over seven years old now. Newer devices may look similar on the outside, but they have better internal computing power and improved physical security.
First and foremost, it’s a phone, but it can also do many useful things like payments, navigation, communication, and taking videos or pictures. It’s great, but nothing truly groundbreaking has been developed in smartphones lately.
I think smartphones have certain limitations in what they can do, and what they can do is already quite useful.
