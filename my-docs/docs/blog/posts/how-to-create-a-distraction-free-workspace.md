# How to create a distraction-free workspace

It takes time. Sometimes, distractions can actually be good—especially if you’re deeply focused and struggling to find an answer. But overall, you need to establish real routines and execute them step by step.
I mostly work on a computer, and my best environment seems to be my home office with good music playing in my headphones. However, I also enjoy working in an office with a separate workspace. As for open offices, I think they can be distracting, but it depends on your state of mind.
My work desk has some clutter on it, but generally, it doesn’t distract me. Perfection doesn’t have to be the key to success.
