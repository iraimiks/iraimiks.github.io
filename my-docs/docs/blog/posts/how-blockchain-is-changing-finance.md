# How blockchain is changing finance

The world is changing, and new terms are entering our lives. It’s hard to imagine how these changes will affect our perspective on a peaceful life—or if they’ll just add more dynamism to our daily existence.
In short, I’m not sure I trust these technological possibilities because I struggle to understand how to start using them without diving deep into their usefulness. It seems I’m stuck in old-school thinking, where finance is my input, my work is the process, and my pay is the output. Not very exciting.
